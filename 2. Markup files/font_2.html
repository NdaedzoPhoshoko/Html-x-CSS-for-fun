What's scones?

Scones are a classic baked treat, often associated with British tea traditions but enjoyed worldwide. They are slightly sweet, biscuit-like pastries made from flour, butter, sugar, and milk or cream, with baking powder as a leavening agent. Scones can be served plain or studded with ingredients like raisins, currants, or dried fruit, and are typically split open and topped with clotted cream, butter, or jam. While traditionally round and slightly crumbly, they can also be shaped into triangles or squares. Perfectly paired with tea or coffee, scones are a versatile and comforting snack, enjoyed at breakfast, tea time, or as a delightful addition to any gathering.
